How to Simply Create an Everyday Eye Look

Natural eye makeup is a staple in the world today. The no-makeup makeup look has been popular for years, continuing to climb up the ladder of success. The best way to achieve this look is by using soft, natural tones such as browns, burgundies, pastel roses, and olive tones, depending on your skin color and desired intensity. Read on to see just how to achieve that perfect everyday eyeshadow look.

Materials

  • Nude eyeshadow
  • ♡ A crease brush
  • ♡ A blending brush
  • ♡ Small and large flat tipped brushes
  • ♡ Mascara

Step 01

Gather all necessary supplies, listed just above this step!

Step 02

Swirl your crease brush into a light transition shade and sweep it over your crease.

Step 03

Using a darker transition color, work it over the outer half of your crease and blend.

Step 04

Using your darkest transition shade, take a small amount with your crease brush and apply only on the outermost edge of your eye. Blend this color into your eyelid as well, 1/4 to 1/2 of the way. This part depends on your desired look.

Step 05

Apply either your middle transition shade or your darkest transition shade halfway under your waterline using your small flat tipped brush for your desired intensity.

Step 06

With the larger flat tipped brush, take a light, shimmery color and apply that to the inner part of your eyelid.

Step 07

Using the same small flat tipped brush from the sixth step, apply the light shimmery color to the inner half of your waterline.

Step 08

Apply your favorite mascara to your lower and upper lashes for desired effect.

Step 09

You now have a natural everyday eye look!
