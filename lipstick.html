How to Effortlessly Apply Liquid Lipstick

Liquid lipsticks are God’s gift to man. Girls, you know my struggle -- eating, drinking, kissing, literally everything involving your lips is an absolute hassle when wearing regular lipstick. And that’s where liquid matte lipsticks come in. Because the lipstick dries down, it is absolutely transfer proof, unless you’re eating anything with lots of oils of course. The downside? Liquid lipstick is probably the most difficult lip product to apply because of the required precision. Read on if you want to see how to flawlessly apply liquid lipstick at home.

Materials

  • Your favorite liquid lipstick
  • ♡ Setting spray
  • ♡ (Optional) A lip brush to clean up the edges

Step 01

Grab your favorite shade of liquid lipstick, and if desired, a lip brush to clean up the edges of your lips.

Step 02

Using the applicator, define your cupid’s bow first at the left, and then at the right. It should look somewhat like a wide, round “M” shape as shown in the picture.

Step 03

Follow the edges of your top lip with the applicator, and if desired, go slightly outside the lips for an overdrawn, dramatic effect.

Step 04

Once you have completed your top lip, sweep your applicator over your bottom lip to fill it in in the same manner as your top lip.

Step 05

If desired, dip your lip brush into the liquid lipstick and clean up the edges of the lips.

Step 06

Spray with a setting spray, and now you have perfect liquid lipstick!
